  2. Space AI: Orbital AI Infrastructure

    The race for AI is a global one, and could lead to vast
    technological advancements in the near and distant future.
    Experts often suggest putting AI infrastructure in space because
    it provides two things for free: energy and cooling. If you can
    imagine putting a ton of solar panels in a large shape in space,
    all connected on each side, the solar panels could be pointed at
    the sun to power the components. Then, if you put the AI
    infrastructure on the opposite side, space is just above zero
    kelvin, so cooling is also free and highly efficient.
